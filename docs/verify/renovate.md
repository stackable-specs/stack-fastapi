## Open follow-ups

- **Rule 10 — KEV/EPSS escalation.** Renovate's `osvVulnerabilityAlerts` surfaces OSV-published CVEs but does not consume CISA KEV or EPSS feeds. The 7-day SLA for known-exploited vulnerabilities (`vulnerability-scanning.md` rule 11) is currently enforced manually during dependency-dashboard triage. Wire a KEV-aware extension (custom Renovate manager or a sibling job in CI that reads the dashboard issue and pages on KEV-listed packages) when the team's alerting infrastructure is in place.

ADR-022 Status remains **Accepted**: every failing rule has a documented carve-out (rules 4, 10, 15) in the ADR's Consequences and a tracked follow-up here.

## How this report was produced

- File-existence and content checks against the stack as of this commit.
- `npx --yes --package renovate -- renovate-config-validator --strict renovate.json` confirms schema conformance.
- `bash verify/adr-022-renovate.sh` runs the rule-by-rule assertions on each commit.
- Re-run on every change to `renovate.json`, `.github/workflows/ci.yml`, `docs/dependencies/renovate.md`, or the source spec.
